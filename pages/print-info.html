Print Info

8x10 inch silver chloride prints are porduced in editions of 50. Prints are processed in archival fashion, spotted and dry mounted on 14x17 inch 4 ply acid free museum board. A beveled window mat of the same material as the mount is applied with linen tape, to reveal the artist's signature in pencil on the lower right corner below the image on the mount and the title on the lower left. Studio stamp and pace in edition on verso of the mount.

PRICE: $150 (plus $10 shipping) Price graduated as the edition sells. Contact the artist to place an order and check on the place in edition. Framing is available for a nominal fee: 14x17 inch, Matte Black Aluminum frames measuring 1.25 inches in depth and 29/32 inches on the face, with a Plexiglas insert, archival foam backer board and wire hanger. Please inquire for current price.
